Steps to Set Up Canon TS3522 Printer

  1. Remove all packing materials from the printer.
  2. Plug in the power cord and turn on the printer.
  3. Lift the scanner unit and wait for the ink cartridge holder to move into view.
  4. Remove the protective packaging from the cartridges.
  5. Insert the cartridges into their respective slots and press them down until they click into place.
  6. Slide out the paper support.
  7. Adjust the paper guides to fit the size of the paper you're using.
  8. Load a stack of plain paper into the rear tray and slide the paper guide snugly against the stack.
  9. Press the Wi-Fi button on the printer until the alarm lamp flashes once.
  10. Press the Color button, then the Wi-Fi button again. The network lamp will start flashing.
  11. Follow the instructions on the printer's LCD screen to connect the printer to your Wi-Fi network.
